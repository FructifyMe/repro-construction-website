Where it landed

Lead hard with the QSR specialty — Dunkin', Jersey Mike's, Dave's Hot Chicken, Wayback Burgers, Smith & Wollensky — instead of the generalist GC framing the current site uses. Your direct competitors (Beam Team, Apex, Asa Carlton) all lead with brand names and project counts. Repro should too.

Bigger picture: the way buyers find contractors is splitting. Google's classic SEO still matters, but the new game is GEO — getting cited by ChatGPT, Google's AI Overviews, Perplexity. Different signal mix entirely (fact density, schema, NAP consistency, AI-readable text). The new site is built for both — fast, low-scroll, concierge-style intake, no kitchen-sink pitch.
