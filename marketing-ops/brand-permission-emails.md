**Purpose:** Three short emails Mark Geissler sends to the three named operators on the new site, asking for written permission to use their name, brand badge, and a 2-4 sentence testimonial.
**Sender:** Mark Geissler (mark@reproconstruction.com). Not Mike. The ask carries owner-to-owner weight from the principal.
**Send window:** Before launch. All three need to be in the site before the launch email goes out to the broader book.
**Note on Richard French:** Richard is the founder/CEO of Works Cafe, not a franchisee. The tone for his email is operator-to-operator at brand-founder level — different from Jason and Philip. The discovery email from Nick had him filed as a "franchisee owner"; public sources confirm otherwise. Mark should know.

Owe you a coffee in Hanover.
