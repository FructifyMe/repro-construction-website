**Owner:** Mark Geissler (from address). Nick Geissler reviews send list weekly.
**Platform:** Built for HubSpot Marketing Hub or Klaviyo Flows. Plain-text only. No HTML, no images, no buttons.
**Personalization tokens:** `{{first_name}}`, `{{brand}}`, `{{city}}`. If `{{brand}}` is empty, replace with "your project."
