Frequently Asked

What homeowners ask before kickoff.

Does Repro still take residential and custom work?

Yes. Custom homes, additions, full renovations, and exterior work — primarily on the North Shore of Massachusetts where we've worked for 30 years. Our restaurant practice gets the marketing oxygen, but residential is half of what we do and the same Mark and Nick run it.

Where does Repro do residential work?

North Shore Massachusetts — Peabody, Danvers, Salem, Beverly, Marblehead, Topsfield, Hamilton, Wenham, and the surrounding towns. Headquarters at 525 Lowell St, Peabody, MA 01960. Outside the North Shore we look at projects case-by-case.

What's the project minimum for a residential renovation?

We're built for substantial work — full renovations, additions, and custom homes — not punch-list calls. If you're planning a project that's structural, multi-room, or full-house, we're the right call. For smaller scope work we'll often refer to a trusted trade partner.

Will Mark or Nick be on my project?

Yes. Owner-side accountability is the same on a residential job as on a restaurant build — Mark and Nick are on the phone, on site, and on the contract. Not a regional PM whose name you'll forget by month three.

Tell us about the house.

Scope, town, what you're trying to do. We'll be back to you in 24 hours with a real answer. Mark or Nick picks up the phone.
