What We Build

From dirt to CO on multi-unit work.

01

Ground-up multifamily

Type-V wood-frame construction in the 8-to-60-unit band. Site, foundation, shell, MEP, finishes, common area, and unit turnover to leasing.

02

Mixed-use

Ground-floor retail or restaurant with residential above. Tenant-fit timing coordinated with residential CO so leasing and store opens don't fight each other.

Podium construction

Concrete podium with wood-frame stacks above — the workhorse multi-family configuration in Greater Boston. Sequencing matters here, and we've done it.

04

Façade & exterior

Façade improvements, envelope upgrades, exterior packages for existing buildings. Coordinated with tenancy and street access on occupied properties.

Frequently Asked

What developers ask before kickoff.

What kinds of multi-unit projects does Repro build?

Ground-up multifamily, mixed-use retail-plus-residential, podium construction, and façade improvement packages across Greater Boston, the North Shore, and into the rest of New England. Owner-side accountability on every project — Mark and Nick are the names on the contract and on the phone.

Does Repro work with private developers and owner-operators?

Yes. Most of our multi-unit work runs with private developers and owner-operators who want a GC that thinks about their pro forma, not just the schedule. We sequence subs against draw schedules, coordinate with leasing and CO targets, and protect the contingency line.

Can Repro handle a mixed-use building with a ground-floor restaurant?

Yes — and this is a sweet spot. Our restaurant practice means a mixed-use building with a ground-floor QSR or full-service tenant gets brand-spec literacy built into the base building. Hood routing, grease interception, MEP risers, and tenant-fit timing are coordinated from schematic, not after the residential CO.

Where does Repro build multi-unit projects?

Greater Boston, the North Shore, and across New England — from Peabody, Salem, Beverly, and Revere down through Boston and the inner-ring suburbs. Headquarters: 525 Lowell St, Peabody, MA 01960.

How big are Repro's multi-unit projects?

Most projects sit in the 8-to-60-unit range — the band where a private developer or owner-operator wants an owner-side GC, not a regional CM that will hand them off to a project engineer. We can scale up, but our differentiator is in this size class.

Tell us what you're building.

Site, unit count, target CO. We'll be back to you in 24 hours with a real answer. Mark or Nick picks up the phone.
