# Brand Permission Email Templates

**Purpose:** Several major franchisors restrict third-party general contractors from publicizing their brand by name without written permission. Featuring "We built X Dunkin' locations" on the new Repro site is great for SEO/GEO — but the franchisor's brand compliance team should be in the loop before launch to avoid a takedown letter or future relationship friction.

**Status:** Templates only. Send order and final list depend on Mark's intake (which brands actually need permission, which are open per his existing relationships).

**Recommendation:** Send these AFTER Mark confirms his completed-brand list and whether he already has informal sign-off from any of his brand reps. For brands where Mark has a personal relationship with the franchisor's construction lead, a phone call beats an email. For brands where Mark is a vendor of a franchisee (and not the franchisor directly), the request goes through whoever the franchisee's official liaison is — Mark will know.

**Sender:** Mark Geissler (mark@reproconstruction.com), not Mike. The ask carries more weight from the principal.
