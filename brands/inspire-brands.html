Inspire Brands FAQ

Questions Inspire Brands operators ask.

Who is an Inspire Brands construction partner in New England?

Repro Construction Services, headquartered at 525 Lowell St in Peabody, MA, builds and remodels Inspire Brands portfolio locations across Massachusetts and the rest of New England. Inspire Brands is a named brand in the Repro portfolio, alongside Dunkin', Jersey Mike's, The Works Cafe, Wayback Burger, Yella, and Zo.

What Inspire Brands portfolio do you build for?

Inspire Brands is the parent of multiple QSR concepts. Repro builds across the portfolio where the brand-spec books align with what we already do — fast-casual line work, drive-thru, brand-finish packages, signage, MEP rough, and equipment set. Contact Repro directly to discuss a specific concept and a specific market.

How long does an Inspire Brands remodel take?

An image-refresh remodel on a portfolio QSR typically runs eight to twelve weeks of active construction once the store is dark, depending on scope, MEP, and equipment lead times. New builds are longer. Repro plans the calendar against a target-open date the operator commits to staffing, marketing, and the landlord.

Where does Repro work for Inspire Brands operators?

All of New England — Massachusetts (North Shore, Greater Boston, MetroWest, South Shore), New Hampshire, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Vermont, and Maine — from our Peabody, MA office.

How does Repro deliver schedule certainty on an Inspire Brands build?

Repro signs off the variance log before mobilization and prices the calendar alongside the construction number. Every closed day on a QSR shell is rent, payroll, and lost royalty income — we don't negotiate the date on the back end.
