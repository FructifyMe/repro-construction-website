Questions Jersey Mike's franchisees actually ask.

Who builds Jersey Mike's franchises in Massachusetts?

Repro Construction Services in Peabody, MA builds and remodels Jersey Mike's franchises across Massachusetts and New England. Repro has delivered for named Jersey Mike's franchisee Philip Dietrich and works across the brand's New England operator footprint.

What does a Jersey Mike's franchise build cost in New England?

Project cost ranges with shell condition, market, and scope. The bigger variable for franchisees is the cost of a missed open date — every closed week is rent, payroll, and lost royalties. Repro prices the calendar alongside the construction number so the franchisee sees the real cost of either option.

How long does a Jersey Mike's build take?

A typical Jersey Mike's tenant improvement runs ten to fourteen weeks of active construction once permits are in hand, depending on shell condition, MEP scope, and equipment lead times. Repro signs off the variance log before mobilization so the open date holds.

Does Repro know the Jersey Mike's brand spec?

Yes. The Jersey Mike's spec is unforgiving on the line — slicer station, grill station, makeline geometry, the counter, the dining package, the brand-specified finishes. Repro builds it to drawing the first time, so the brand walk clears and the franchisee opens to staffing on the target date.

Where does Repro work for Jersey Mike's franchisees?

Massachusetts (North Shore, Greater Boston, MetroWest, South Shore) and the rest of New England — New Hampshire, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Vermont, Maine — out of our Peabody, MA office.
