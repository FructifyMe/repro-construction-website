Works Cafe FAQ

Questions operators ask before the bid.

Who is The Works Cafe's construction partner in New England?

Repro Construction Services, based in Peabody, MA, is The Works Cafe's construction partner in New England. Repro built the Westford, MA location for Richard French, founder and CEO of The Works Cafe.

Where is The Works Cafe Westford located?

The Westford, MA Works Cafe sits on Route 110. Repro delivered the ground-up new build to brand spec, and the store opened on the date the operator put on the calendar.

How long does a Works Cafe build take?

A Works Cafe new build, the kind Repro delivered in Westford, runs roughly twenty to twenty-eight weeks from mobilization to first customer, depending on shell, MEP, and equipment lead times. Repro plans the calendar to a target-open date the operator can commit to staffing and marketing.

Does Repro build for The Works Cafe outside Westford?

Yes — Repro serves The Works Cafe brand across all of New England (Massachusetts, New Hampshire, Rhode Island, Connecticut, Vermont, Maine) from our Peabody, MA office. Richard French's endorsement is on record.

What is special about The Works Cafe brand spec?

The Works Cafe's order counter, bake case, bagel display, and café floor flow are the brand signatures. Repro builds them to drawing so the operator inherits a clean room — no rework on day-one walk-throughs.

How do I reach Repro about a Works Cafe project?

Use the concierge intake on this page or call Mark Geissler at (617) 820-8681. Richard French is also a named reference.
